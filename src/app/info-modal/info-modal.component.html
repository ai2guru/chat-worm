Advantages of Chatworm over ChatGPT Client:
  • Availability: Even when ChatGPT client is down.
  • Faster Response Time: Minimize delays and no too many requests in 1h.
  • Cost-Effective: Cheaper than ChatGPT Plus.
  • Multiple Models: Including models with no content policy (ex. text-davinci-003).
  • Customizable: Change the randomness etc.
  • More Features: The application comes with many more features.
  • Open Source: Github Repo
